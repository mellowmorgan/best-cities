Where is the best city for you?

We use current data to find the best US city to live in according to your occupation.

Info

Best City helps users find the perfect city for them based upon their occupation, using salary and rental costs to determine the best U.S. cities to live in.

About

Best City was developed by Morgan Waites using Flask, a Python web framework. Salary and occupational data was obtained from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. Rental data was obtained from Zillow through the Quandl API.
